Make wine your way — solo or together

Whether you’re creating your personal vintage or blending with a group, each visit brings your story to life through crushing, pressing, racking, and custom bottling your own wines.

For Individual Wine Lovers

Craft a signature vintage from grape to glass with hands-on guidance. Choose your varietal, join key sessions — crush, press, rack — then design your label and take home bottles you made yourself.

For Friends, Families & Teams

Make it a shared vintage. Reserve a batch, pick your grapes, and return across the season for each milestone — ending with a celebratory bottling party where everyone leaves with custom-labeled wine.
